About Me

I completed my master's degree in Artificial Intelligence at Universitas Syiah Kuala. Currently, I am a lecturer in the Department of Information Systems, Faculty of Engineering, at Universitas Abulyatama, Aceh Besar, Indonesia. My research primarily focuses on the implementation and improvement of QSRR and QSAR models using various machine learning algorithms. In addition to this, I am actively involved in other exciting fields such as deep learning for image recognition and forecasting, molecular docking, chemo-informatics, and econometrics.
